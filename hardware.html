Hardware and Software used for website development, including the cost incurred

2010s: Advances in technology and the shift towards more complex website designs and functionalities required higher-spec computers and software, including IDEs and CMSs. prices drop still and everything technology wise becomes cheaper

2020s: The emergence of new technology such as artificial intelligence and machine learning is requiring developers to have more advanced hardware and software to keep up with the latest trends and technologies.
