Fortnite is a popular battle royale video game developed by Epic Games. The game involves up to 100 players dropping onto an island, gathering weapons, resources, and building structures to survive and eliminate other players until only one is left standing. The game also includes various game modes, challenges, and events, and has a colorful, cartoonish art style. It has become a global phenomenon, attracting millions of players of all ages and even spawning a competitive esports scene.
